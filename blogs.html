act #1

the road drive of a life time

As i drive my broken down rusted and busted vehicle, down the isolated chaos that arranged together in a peaceful way, it was warm even though it was winter.

the cold cold hug of the winter held my car as the floods of snow had to be driven into and away from, an animal appered in the corner of my eye, it was a deer.
just watching me i singal it away and it runs away with its shoe looking feet, the fur moving in the non-existent air

the snow now starting to clear up and i drive my car into the cracked road which looks like it has not been driven on for 15 years.

i could smell the moisture in the air from the melted snow as the bright yellow ball arised from the west.

now without any snow or any sun to guide me, all alone i keep on driving.
i see a small farm with kids playing at night, ducking and hiding in the wheat.

i feel a sense of calm while driving past the kids, taking me back to the time when i used to play hide and seek with my friends


i spot another farm nearby, its day 2 right about now and the sun is starting to show up to the party late again.

i drive my car all swrivy and swerly
until i reach the farm, i get out of my car and sit on a hay stack looking up at the sky. the peace of the moment sets in like a warm apple pie in winter
